# Contributing to HizenLabs.Plugins

Contributions are welcome: bug fixes, improvements, and new plugins.

## The CLA

These plugins are open source under the GPLv3. Before a change can be merged, we ask
contributors to agree to our [Contributor License Agreement](https://gist.github.com/EthanDelong/7e7e19ea155c4dfc6d0317747629c1ed),
the same org-wide CLA used across all HizenLabs projects. It lets you keep your
copyright while granting Aerial Byte LLC the rights to maintain the project's
licensing. A bot will prompt you to sign on your first pull request.

## Workflow

1. Open an issue for anything non-trivial so we can align on the approach first.
2. Keep changes focused; match the surrounding style.
3. Open a PR describing what changed and why.
